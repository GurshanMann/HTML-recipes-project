Burgers

  1. Heat up frozen jane chicken burgers for 9 minutes each side in the airfryer at 400 degrees farenhite
  2. Wash and dry a handful of iceberg lettuce
  3. Obtain your choice of burger buns and lather your choice of sauce on each end
  4. Assemble the burger with lettuce meat and sauce, include any other ingredients as you please

Enjoy!
