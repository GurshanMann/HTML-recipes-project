Pizza

  1. Get your choice of homemade or premade dough and kneed to the desired length
  2. Apply your choice of homestyle pizza sauce and lather around the dough leaving an inch off the end
  3. Add your favorite ingredients to the pizza
  4. Add shredded mozzeralla cheese
  5. Preheat the oven to bake at 400 degrees farenhite
  6. Once the oven is preheated place the pizza into the oven for 11 minutes
  7. Once time is up take the pizza out and let it rest for 4 minutes
  8. Enjoy your pizza your favourite sauce

Enjoy!
