Chicken Wings

  1. Prepare 1 lbs of chicken wings by washing and rinsing with warm water
  2. Season chicken wings with your favorite choice of seasoning
  3. Let it rest for 2 hours to let the marinate sink in
  4. Preheat Air fryer for 4 mins on 450 F
  5. Place chicken wings in and cook at 400 F for 8 minutes
  6. After the 8 minutes is up flip the chicken wings and repeat for another 8 minutes
  7. Take out the chicken wings and let rest for 4 minutes

Enjoy!
